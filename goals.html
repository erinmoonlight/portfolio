Goals

After graduating from the MFA program, I hope to continue working in publishing.


I would love to learn about other areas of publishing, such as marketing, publicity, production and editorial.


One potential goal I have is developing a part-time freelance business. As a publishing professional, I could assist self-published writers and small companies with rights and intellectual property management.

I will probably still need a real job.


As a writer, I would love to finish my novel and seek publication. My stretch goals are a short story publication in the New York Times and a Pushcart Prize nomination.
